Facial Recognition

This Biometric Security system is capable of identifying an individual based on facial features such as the iris.

Benefits

  • The iris is the coloured area around the eye and a special scanner can capture these patterns and turn them into data to help with identification increase the accuracy, convenience and speed of authentication.
  • Facial features, such as the iris have many individuating characteristics. Even more than the fingerprint!

Costs

  • People can use special devices to change their face shape, which may be able to fool a system.
  • The biometric security system has difficulty differentiation between identical twins.
