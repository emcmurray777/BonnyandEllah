Voice Recognition

This Biometric Security system compares characteristics like voice pitch, intensity and dynamics to determine the identity of an individual.

Benefits

  • Enables people with disabilities to operate computers.
  • Increases efficiency for those who are not able to type well or fast.

Costs

  • May mix up homonyms and their spelling.
  • Can be affected by background noises such as those found on a crowded street, airplane or public transportation.
  • Users may be deterred from using for fear that others will overhear personal information.
