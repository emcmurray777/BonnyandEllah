Fingerprints

Mobile devices play a significant role in everyday life. They have become an essential means for communication, entertainment and the maintenance of social relationships. With the increase in mobile device usage, the amount of confidential data has grown; passwords for bank accounts, personal emails accounts, and media are all affected so. Consequently, there has been an increase in confidential data which is now being protected via Biometric Security systems.
The use of Biometric Security systems is increasing rapidly without discussion or consideration of many of the ethical issues involved. Fingerprints is a site is a sign designed to spread awareness of the true costs, benefits, and ethical concerns of implementing Biometric Security in daily life.
